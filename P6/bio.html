Bio Information

Colin F. Han

My name is Colin F. Han. After being a software engineer for more than five years, I'm now a master's student majoring in Computer Engineering at Northwestern University.
Before coming to Northwestern, in my position as a Principal Software Engineer for the Research and Development Center of the Agricultural Bank of China, I designed and developed several web systems, such as the Intelligent Knowledge Management System, and Online Customer Service Platform. And I have worked extensively with React, Spring Boot, and Docker. I'm now a master's student and also a developer at the Center for Talent Development at NU.
